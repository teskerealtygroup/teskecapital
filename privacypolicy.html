Section 01

Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through the Teske Capital website, related contact forms, email communications, and other digital interactions controlled by Teske Capital.

This policy does not govern websites, platforms, applications, or services operated independently by third parties.

Section 02

Information you provide

Teske Capital may collect information that you voluntarily provide when submitting a contact form, expressing future investor interest, sending an email, or otherwise communicating with the firm.

This information may include:

  • First and last name.
  • Email address.
  • Telephone number.
  • State, country, or general location.
  • Inquiry type and message contents.
  • Potential investment range or timeline, when voluntarily provided.
  • Accredited-investor status or related eligibility information, when voluntarily provided.
  • Referral source and communication preferences.
  • Any other information included in your message or correspondence.
Section 03

Information collected automatically

When you visit the website, certain information may be collected automatically through hosting providers, server logs, security tools, analytics services, or similar technologies.

Automatically collected information may include:

  • Internet Protocol address.
  • Browser type and version.
  • Device type and operating system.
  • Date and time of access.
  • Referring and exit pages.
  • Pages viewed and basic navigation activity.
  • Approximate geographic region.
  • Security, error, and diagnostic information.

This information may be used to maintain the website, diagnose technical issues, detect misuse, improve performance, and protect the website and its users.

Section 04

How information may be used

Teske Capital may use collected information for purposes including:

  • Responding to questions and inquiries.
  • Recording nonbinding expressions of future investor interest.
  • Providing requested information or website updates.
  • Maintaining business and communication records.
  • Improving website content, usability, security, and performance.
  • Preventing fraud, abuse, spam, and unauthorized access.
  • Complying with legal, regulatory, accounting, tax, or compliance obligations.
  • Establishing, exercising, or defending legal rights.
Section 05

Contact and investor-interest submissions

The contact page may allow visitors to submit general inquiries or express nonbinding interest in receiving information about Teske Capital in the future.

Submitting a form does not create an investment, advisory, fiduciary, client, or contractual relationship. It also does not constitute an investment commitment, subscription, offer, acceptance, or guarantee of future access.

The website contact form is currently in development mode.

Before public launch, form submissions may be processed through Teske Capital’s own server infrastructure and an email delivery provider such as Mailgun. This policy may be updated when the final production system is implemented.

Section 06

Cookies, analytics, and similar technologies

Teske Capital may use cookies, local storage, analytics technologies, or similar tools to support website functionality, understand general usage, improve performance, and protect the website.

These technologies may be used for:

  • Remembering basic site preferences.
  • Measuring aggregate website traffic.
  • Identifying technical errors.
  • Preventing abuse and malicious activity.
  • Improving navigation and page performance.

Visitors may adjust browser settings to block or delete cookies. Certain features may not function properly if cookies or local-storage technologies are disabled.

Section 07

How information may be shared

Teske Capital does not intend to sell or rent personal information.

Information may be disclosed to:

  • Website hosting, infrastructure, email, security, analytics, and technology service providers.
  • Attorneys, accountants, consultants, or other professional advisors.
  • Government, regulatory, judicial, or law enforcement authorities when required by applicable law or valid legal process.
  • Parties involved in a potential business transaction, restructuring, acquisition, financing, or transfer of assets.

Service providers may receive only the information reasonably necessary to perform services on behalf of Teske Capital and may be subject to contractual or legal confidentiality obligations.

Section 08

Data retention

Teske Capital may retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to inquiries, maintain business records, protect legal rights, comply with legal or regulatory obligations, and support legitimate business purposes.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information, the reason it was collected, applicable legal requirements, and whether an ongoing communication or business relationship exists.

Section 09

Data security

Teske Capital intends to use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, misuse, or destruction.

Safeguards may include encrypted communications, access controls, server monitoring, software updates, authentication, backups, firewall protections, and security logging.

No website, server, email system, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Visitors should avoid submitting highly sensitive information through ordinary website forms or email.

Section 10

Your privacy choices and rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights concerning your personal information, including the right to:

  • Request access to information maintained about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of certain information.
  • Withdraw consent to certain communications.
  • Opt out of nonessential promotional emails.
  • Request information about how your data has been used or disclosed.

Teske Capital may need to verify your identity before processing a privacy request. Certain information may be retained when required by law or necessary for legitimate legal, regulatory, security, or recordkeeping purposes.

Section 11

Third-party services and links

This website may use or link to third-party services such as hosting providers, email delivery services, analytics platforms, market-data services, social networks, or external websites.

Teske Capital does not control the privacy, security, or data-handling practices of independent third parties. Visitors should review the applicable privacy policies of those services before providing personal information.

Section 12

Children’s privacy

The Teske Capital website is intended for adults and is not directed to children under the age of 13.

Teske Capital does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such information is identified, it may be deleted within a reasonable period.

Section 13

Changes to this policy

Teske Capital may update this Privacy Policy as the website, business, legal structure, technology systems, or information practices change.

Updated policies may be posted on this page without individual notice. The effective date displayed at the top of the page identifies when the policy was last materially revised.

Section 14

Questions, correction requests, deletion requests, and other privacy inquiries may be submitted through the Teske Capital contact page.
